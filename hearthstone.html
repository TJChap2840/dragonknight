Hearthstone Tournament

Date:

  • Weekend of January 17th (date may change)
  • Sign up on guild calendar

Format:

  • Single elimination tournament bracket
  • Each match is a best of 3
  • 5000 gold entry fee
  • Bracket and payouts will be announced the week of the tournament

Rules:

  • Select 2 decks that will be used throughout the tournament
  • Decks must be sent in before the bracket is announced
  • Decks cannot be modified after being sent in
  • During a match, you cannot play a deck again after you have won with it
  • Entry fee must be sent in before play begins
  • Automatic forfeit will occur if you do make your match unless advanced notice given
  • Automatic forfeit will occur if your deck is modified after deck submission time

Additional Info:

  • I will attempt to have a stream available for all matches
  • All players must be willing to add tournament organizers to their battle.net friends list
  • I will need additional streamers to help with spectating games
